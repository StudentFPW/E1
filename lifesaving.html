LIFESAVING


THE MOST IMPORTANT PHILOSOPHY OF THE RESCUE SERVICE IS TO STOP THE EMERGENCY BEFORE IT HAPPENS

Life Savings are very responsible and you should take care of them like a small child.
Every second you must be attentive to every movement of these people.

ABCABS Rescuer Rule ! The most important rule !
  1. Airways open
  2. Breath normally
  3. Chest compression
  4. Airways open
  5. Breath to patient
  6. (Serious bleeding, Spinal injury, Shock)

We will not stop these points until we return life, we repeat and repeat these points

This is the rule we use if someone is drowning or in cardiac shock. The last point (6) we use after the return life.
